FAQs

Every Royal Enfield Classic is an expression of the brands DNA in an elegant, undiluted form. These machines embody the spirit of Pure Motorcycling. The Classic 650 offers riders a powerful blend of old-school aesthetics and effortless power delivery. Its stately, streamlined form is propelled by Royal Enfields renowned 650 engine, resulting in a ride that is both swift and graceful.

The Classic 650 continues the legacy of the Classic series, which has been the purest expression of Royal Enfields DNA. It draws inspiration from Royal Enfields first-ever Parallel Twin, the 500 Twin (1948), while still retaining the timeless elegance, craftsmanship, and old-school charm of the Classic lineup.

  • The new Classic 650 also comes with a host of features providing enhanced safety, comfort and convenience.
  • LED Headlamps and Pilot Light: The hallmark headlamp of the Classic 650 returns with improved illumination and increased efficiency, offering enhanced visibility while staying unapologetically stylish.
  • LED Turn Indicators: Old-school halogen indicators make way for higher visibility LED indicators, keeping you safe whether youre leaning left or riding right.
  • Adjustable Clutch and Brake Levers: An adjustable span and improved ergonomics on the new Classic 650s clutch and brake levers allow riders of every size and skill to manoeuver their motorcycle with confidence. 
  • USB Charging Port: All the iconic styling cues of a 1950s motorcycle without the 1950s drawbacks. Stay connected to the 21st century with a USB charging port for all your device.
  • Gear Position Indicator: The new Classic 650s updated instrument cluster includes a gear position indicator that improves gear management, prevents engine strain and ensures the smoothest of gear shifts.
  • Tripper: The new Classic 650s comes with a Tripper navigation display unit that helps you ride hassle free without having to attend to your smartphone ensuring an enjoyable & comfortable ride.
